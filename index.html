Hospice Care

We believe

We believe in death with dignity. We believe that the most valuable resource in hospice care is your attention. We believe the right tools can knit the care team together with less focus on technology, not more.

We believe a less distracted and more meaningful point of care experience strengthens the entire hospice organization.

We simplify

As we move into the 21st century, both the opportunities and challenges for end-of-life care continue to evolve. The requirements of federal compliance, the time-consuming nature of current electronic medical records, and the training and re-training of clinical staff can all take a toll on the mission to deliver thoughtful, focused end-of-life care.

Much of the software we are forced to use directs our attention away from the faces we’re caring for and onto the screens that should be secondary. Records are kept at the expense of a hospice organization’s most valuable resource: the time and attention of its care team.

Bloom was designed to simplify the point-of-care experience for everyone involved. Our absolute goal is to reduce the amount of time spent inputting information into software, using technology to actually increase your ability to touch your patients so you can truly stay in constant contact.

We improve

Bloom complements, simplifies, and improves the EMR and administrative systems that you already use, regardless of vendor or solution.

Bloom provides a single place on the tablet or mobile device of the patient, family and clinical staff to participate more meaningfully at the point of care. Video calls and text messages allow the patient, family, and care givers to stay in constant contact, all of which is automatically recorded and appended to the patient’s EMR.

When communication is recorded, record keeping happens automatically and with higher fidelity, giving hospice staff more time for truly patient-centric care.
